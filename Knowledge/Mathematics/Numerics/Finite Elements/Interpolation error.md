## Interpolation error
Let $T$ be a simplex and $I_h$ the nodal interpolation with respect to its vertices. Then for all $v \in H^2(T)$, we have
	$||\nabla (v- I_h v)||_{L^2(T)} \leq C ||D^2 v ||_{L^2(T)}$
