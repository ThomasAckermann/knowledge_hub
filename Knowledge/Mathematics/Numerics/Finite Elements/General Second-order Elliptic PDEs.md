## Problem Statement and weak solution
The Poisson model problem is a special case of a more general scalar second-order linear elliptic partial differential equations of the form:
	$- \text{div} (A \nabla u) + b \cdot \nabla u + c u = f$
The weak solution is then given by
	$\int_\Omega (A \nabla u) \nabla v + (b \nabla u) v + cuv \text{d}x = \int_\Omega fv \text{d}x + \int_{\Gamma_N} z v \text{d}s$
