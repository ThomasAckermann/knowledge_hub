The pseudoinverse is a generalization of the inverse matrix to singular and non-square matrices $A \in \mathbb{m \times n}$. It is also called the Moore-Penrose Pseudoinverse. 
$A^+ = A^\top (A A^\top)^{-1}$ 

The following apply:
- $(A^+)^+$ = A
- $(A^\top)^+ = (A^+)^\top$
- $(\lambda A )^+ = \lambda^{-1} A^+$
