## Definition
A signal $x(t)$ is analytic if $X(i \omega) =0$ for $\omega < 0$.
## Kramers-Kroning Relations
A signal $x(t) = r(t) + \text{i} i(t)$ is analytic if and only if
	$i(t) = \hat{r(t)}$, $r(t)=- \hat{i}(t)$
