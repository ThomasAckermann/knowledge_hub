The interation matrix or [[Image]] jacobian ($L$) describes the relationship between the velocity of an [[Image]] feature and the camera. 
	$\dot{s} = L(u,v,z) \dot{p}$ 

- $\dot{s}$ velocity of a pixel
- $\dot{p}$ velocity of the camera
- $z$ depth of a point
- $L \in \mathbb{R}^{2x6}$
